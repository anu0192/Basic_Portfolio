About Me

Hello! I'm Anusree Sarkar, and I hope everything is going well for you.
I’m a Tech Enthusiast, Aspiring Software Engineer, and an Open-Source Contributor who genuinely believes in learning by building. For me, technology is not just about writing code — it’s about creating solutions that make things simpler, faster, and more meaningful. Over the past few years, I’ve taken classroom concepts and transformed them into real, functional projects — from interactive weather apps to complete full-stack systems. While working on these, I’ve explored how things actually operate behind the scenes: how data flows, how APIs work, how UI affects user experience, and how different parts of a system connect together. I love experimenting with new technologies, improving the things I’ve already built, and polishing every detail until it feels right. With every project, I focus on writing clean, understandable code and building user-friendly interfaces that make an impact. My long-term goal is to grow into a developer who thinks beyond syntax — someone who can design systems, understand user needs, collaborate effectively, and build solutions that truly help people. Every day, I try to learn something new, explore a new idea, or refine a skill, and I genuinely enjoy that journey.
